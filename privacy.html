Privacy Policy

At Layers Chain™, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you connect a wallet, we do not collect any information. This data is encrypted client-side and never stored in our database.
  • Non-Personal Information: We collect usage data such as IP addresses (hashed), browser type, and interaction logs to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Facilitate wallet connections to decentralized applications (dApps).
  • Ensure security through HTTPS encryption and client-side processing.
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience (anonymized data only).

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmissions are protected by HTTPS.
  • Credentials are encrypted client-side using AES encryption before connection.
  • We do not store your private keys or seed phrases on our servers cos we can't.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third party, and we do not store your web activities.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Understand how your data is processed (as outlined here).
  • Contact us to request clarification or deletion of any stored data (see below).

6. Cookies

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies beyond basic analytics (anonymized).
